Data description

Whole grinds of collected mosquitoes are tested for arbovirus nucleic acids to determine the presence of arboviruses in mosquitoes.

  • The map shows detections of indictors across locations for a reporting week.
  • Charts allow comparison of an indicator across locations, or indicators within a location
  • The table allows export of the selected data

Mosquito are trapped and counted across sites within locations, and the location average presented. Culex annulirostris and Aedes vigilax are vectors of interest for Ross River virus and Barmah Forest virus, Culex annulirostris is also a vector for Japanese encephalitis virus.

  • The map shows averaged counts across locations for a reporting week.
  • Charts allow comparison of an indicator across locations, or indicators within a location
  • The table allows export of the selected data
