These displays provide the latest virus detection results from trapped mosquitos and sentinel chickens. Whole grinds of collected mosquitoes are tested for arbovirus nucleic acids to determine the presence of arboviruses in mosquitoes, while chickens are bled for detection of virus antibodies indicating exposure to these viruses.
